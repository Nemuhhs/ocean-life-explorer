Dolphins

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and remarkable communication skills. They live in social groups and use echolocation to navigate and hunt.

Quick Facts

  • They can swim up to 20 mph.
  • Dolphins communicate using clicks and whistles.
  • They have excellent long-term memory.
