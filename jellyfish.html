Jellyfish

Jellyfish are ancient marine animals that have existed for more than 500 million years. They drift through the oceans using their umbrella-shaped bells and trailing tentacles.

Life Cycle Stages

  1. Planula
  2. Polyp
  3. Ephyra
  4. Medusa (Adult)
